Serverless Setup

An interactive walkthrough covering encryption, jurisdictional restrictions, business continuity, and the Cloudflare Data Localization Suite (DLS).

Data Security

Organizations must ensure data is encrypted at rest and in transit, with auditable key management and no plaintext exposure.

Jurisdictional Control

Regulations require certain data to stay within specific geographies. Developers need built-in ways to restrict storage (data-at-rest) by region.

Business Continuity

Compliance frameworks mandate resilient architectures with failover, backups, and guaranteed uptime even during partial outages.

DLS Add-on

Enterprises need granular technical control over where TLS keys, HTTP inspection, and metadata processing and storage occur within the network.

Why does this matter?
Political tension and evolving regulations mean compliance-sensitive organizations need configurable, region-aware serverless setups. Key frameworks include NIS2, CRA, PCI DSS, FIPS, and CISA.
